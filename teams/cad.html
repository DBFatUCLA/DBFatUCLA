Computer-Aided Design Subteam


Development Responsibilities

The CAD subteam builds a 3D model for the plane based off of the sizing from the aerodynamics subteam using Solidworks. This is then used to put the plane together.


Plane Development

The CAD design provides a preview of the plane before it is built. The team develops conceptual designs into detailed planns, allowing for the manufacturing of components. The design is used to create jigs and molds for the creation of parts, as well as for some parts to be 3D-printed from the design directly.
