Manufacturing Subteam



Development Responsibilities

The Manufacturing team is responsible for constructing the plane. This includes wet carbon fiber layups, 3D printed frameworks, laser-cut balsa, and innovative designs that are catered specifically to our plane.


Flight Day Responsibilities

On Fly Days, it is essential that the manufacturing subteam ensures that the plane is ready to fly; some responsibilities include assembling the modular components, connecting wiring, and covering all hatches securely. Packing lists also need to be written and followed so that we have all necessary tools and equipment to repair the plane at the airfield if necessary.



Should I Join?

Manufacturing involves hands-on work, thinking outside the box, and (sometimes) last minute fixes and repairs. If you're interested by the idea of physically constructing our competition plane, then this subteam is definitely for you!
