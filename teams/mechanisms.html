Mechanisms

The Mechanisms Subteam will be responsible for designing, testing, and implementing major mechanism subsystems into the aircraft. The primary focus will be mechanisms relating to the main mission goals of the competition (e.g. syringe loading & package deployment for 2021-2022). The Mechanisms Lead will host the subteam meetings, facilitate design discussions, and delegate tasks necessary for the design progression of each specific mechanism.
