Aerodynamics Subteam


Development Responsibilities

The Aerodynamics subteam is tasked with estimating how plane design influences scores, selecting the best plane cofiguration, and creating dimensions for the plane.


Plane Configuration

A variety of different configurations are considered for the plane. Decisions made include plane styles (single wing, biplane, lifting body), win placement, landing gear configuration, propulsion configuration, and more!


Plane Sizing

To size the plane, a Matlab code evaluates potential choices based on stability and expected mission scores to find the optimal sizes for a given plane configuration.
