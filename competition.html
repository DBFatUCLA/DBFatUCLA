Competition 2022



The Missions

Each year, the DBF competition rules set three missions and one ground mission. Each mission has its own criteria on how to earn points; we build a plane to maximize the total points over all missions. This year, the missions are as follows:

  • Mission 1: Flight Mission - Demonstrate ability to fly unloaded plane
  • Mission 2: Cargo Mission - Carry as many syringes as possible
  • Mission 3: Vaccine Package Deployment - Deploy vaccine vial packages on the runway
  • Ground Mission - Load and unload plane

Logistics

Competition was held in Wichita, Kansas on April 21 to 24 2022. We drove out with the plane from Westwood and stayed in the area for competition. We are now waiting for the 2022-2023 Competition Rules to be released!
