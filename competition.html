The Missions

Each year, the DBF competition rules set three flight missions and one ground mission. Each mission has its own criteria on how to earn points; we build a plane to maximize the total points over all missions. This year, the missions are as follows:

  • Mission 1: Aircraft Delivery - Demonstrate ability to fly unloaded plane for three laps in five minutes or less.
  • Mission 2: Medical Transportation - Carry a payload of wooden peg dolls symbolizing the crew, patient on a gurney, and a medical supply cabinet.
  • Mission 3: Urban Taxiing - Carry a payload of wooden peg dolls symbolizing passengers.
  • Ground Mission: Configuration Demonstration - Transition between parking configuration and the flight configurations of each mission, as fast as possible.
