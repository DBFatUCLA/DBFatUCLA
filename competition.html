The Missions

Each year, the DBF competition rules set three flight missions and one ground mission. Each mission has its own criteria on how to earn points; we build a plane to maximize the total points over all missions. This year, the missions are as follows:

  • Mission 1: Aircraft Delivery - The mission is to demonstrate the aircraft's ability to fly three laps in under five minutes, without a payload.
  • Mission 2: Medical Transportation - The mission is to fly three laps as quickly as possible, while carrying a payload of wooden peg dolls symbolizing the crew, patient on a gurney, and a medical supply cabinet.
  • Mission 3: Urban Taxiing - The mission is to fly as many laps as possible in five minutes, while carrying a payload of wooden peg dolls symbolizing passengers.
  • Ground Mission: Configuration Demonstration - The mission is to transition between parking configuration and the flight configurations, with the M2 and M3 payloads, as fast as possible.
